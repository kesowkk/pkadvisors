About CA KK Padmanabhan

CA KK Padmanabhan, Founder of PK Advisors, is a Chartered Accountant and senior consulting professional with extensive, hands-on experience in GST advisory, GST litigation and representation, Company Law compliance, Accounting Standards/Financial Reporting, international taxation, and privacy advisory for businesses and individuals.

Over the course of his practice, he has spent 20,000+ hours in consulting and client-facing advisory work. He has appeared across 200+ GST offices and has represented 1,000+ litigation matters, with cumulative dispute resolution/settlement value reported at ₹1,500 crore+. His work is grounded in disciplined documentation, practical strategy, and clear client communication—especially where timelines, evidence, and positions must stand scrutiny.

Alongside practice, he has delivered 10,000+ hours of lectures on GST, Company Law, and Accounting Standards. He has assisted in authoring books on Financial Reporting and contributed to four books on Data Privacy.

He is also proficient in AI-assisted professional tools to enhance research, drafting, and workflow efficiency—while emphasizing confidentiality, controlled usage, and privacy-aware practices. His privacy advisory focuses on translating requirements into implementable steps—policies, processes, and safeguards—without unnecessary complexity.

PK Advisors is supported by a team of experts and working associates across its service lines, enabling timely execution and dependable client support for both routine compliance and complex representation matters.
